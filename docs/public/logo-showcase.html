Design Concept

The "Avocado Chip" logo concept merges two ideas:

  • Avocado cross-section: The miniature-guacamole name and playful nature
  • Microprocessor die: The AI/technical foundation of the framework
  • Network connections: Six radiating lines represent the 20-agent system working together
  • Organic + Technical: Rounded, friendly shapes with precise circuit patterns

The logo works at all sizes—from 16px favicons to large displays—maintaining clarity and brand recognition.
